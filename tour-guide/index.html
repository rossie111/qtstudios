I like to break up the tour into 3 parts:

  1. orient to space set up and shared gear -- i like to start with the drums in the back and then work my way to the front/ couch;
  2. sit down to chat on the couch and get to know the person, share about myself and connection to the studio, answer and any questions;
  3. wrap up the tour by showing them the fire escape, backyard, and bathrooms. 


Part 1 - shared gear

  • show where all the shared gear is 
    • Shared music equipment
      • drum set
      • cables
      • mic stands
      • a mixer sound board
      • speakers
    • Shared photo/video equipment
      • Green/black/white screen
      • Professional photo lights


Part 2 - chitchat on couch

  • give background of space and ethos/values/intetions/visions; 

    • prioritize access to queer/bipoc; solo artist

    • shared space with 7 other solo artist and one band of 4 people -- try to keep it max at 12 people sharing the space

    • every person get ~6 hours --> normally the calendar is very open and folks are welcome to go over this time if they're working on a special project or don't see anyone on the calendar--just need to check in with studiomates on the group chat--

      • the 6 hours per solo artist/band is set up so that in case there is a time when the space is being highly utilized, we can have some parameters for sharing the space in an equitable and fair way.  

    • 24 hour access

    • can be as loud as you want --most liberating part of being in the space for me

    • value open communication and nonhierachal use of space

    • security depost --> $120 ( a months rent plus cost of keys if not returned)

    • lease is month to month, with a 40 day notice to leave-- ideally given 10 days before you're last month

      • for example: you last month is june so you would give notice May 20th

  • get a sense of who the person is and how they'll be using the space

    • what kind of music/ art do they make?

    • what do they do for work?

  • Answer any questions 

  • Next steps after tour 

    • message to confirm your interest in being a studiomate asap

    • rossie will reach back out after the tour to let you know if you've been selected and if so then scheduling a time this weekend/before march 1 to sign contract + give keys

    • Add you to group chat + g calendar


Part 3 - wrapping up/ fire escape, backyard, & bathrooms

  • show fire escape
