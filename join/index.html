Opportunities to get involved with QT Studios!

QT Studios has two opportunities to get involved with the space and organization.

✩Studiomate Opening!✩

We currently have an opening for a bipoc studiomate! Preference given to visual (photo/video) artists. Please fill out our interest form at https://qtstudios415.com/join

✩Steering Committee Launch!✩

For the next 5 to 7 months we will be developing the foundation for QT Studios to become a cooperative business structure in order to expand our impact in SF art and culture, and support for local independent artists. We are looking for 3 passionate musicians, artists, and community members that work at the intersection of art, culture, and social justice to join these conversations and become stewards of QT Studios' vision. Apply at https://qtstudios415.com/steering-committee
